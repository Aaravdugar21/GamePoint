Welcome to GamePoint

Welcome to GamePoint! Here, you can create and find pickup games to join. Join our community to start taking your pickup games to the next level. If you are interested in a demo, please contact us and we'll try to provide a demo as best as we can. Thanks for using GamePoint to take your pickup games to the next level.

Currently, our GamePoint family has reached over 800 members and growing, across states like New York and Texas. We have raised over $1k to fund its mission to bridge the gap between the player and game and are persistent in our efforts to improve pickup sports.

You can create a pickup game for any sport. If there's a sport that you're interested in but it's not an option, please email sharpkeen88@gmail.com and we'll try to add your sport as soon as possible.

Pricing

As of now, creating and joining pickup games is completely free. Feel free to create as many pickup games as you'd like. We might add a subscription feature down the road but if you already have an account, you're good for now!
